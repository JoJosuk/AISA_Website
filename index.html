What We Do

Learning opportunity

In this fast-paced era, it's essential for us to be aware of the different space technologies out there. There's so much to learn and see!

Outreach opportunity

Our club can organize outreach events and share our passion with the community around us in our locality.

Personal growth

We encourage our members to step outside their comfort zones. We hope to provide various events to help them develop their knowledge and confidence.

Host seminars & workshops

Different seminars and workshops on astronomy will be held in our campus to raise awareness ahout it.

Space Hacks

Our Space hackathons have enabled students to collaborate and generate innovative products and ideas that push the limits of space exploration and innovation.

Space Events

The club organizes various space-related activities such as quizzes and exhibitions providing opportunities for students to participate and engage in space-related events.
